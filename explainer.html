Leadership style & leading through uncertainty

Outcome‑driven, calm under pressure, high‑context comms. During a Lakehouse cutover with conflicting priorities, I ran a 2‑hour RACI+risks workshop, aligned on a thin‑slice MVP, added blue/green backout and parallel validation. Go‑live on schedule, zero P1s, clear ownership.

Critical decisions framework

Guardrail metrics (SLA, spend, reliability) + short decision memos (options, risks, blast radius). For cluster policy, compared three configs by cost/SLO impact; chose balanced spec with workload‑aware autoscaling; approved in one review.

Speed vs. quality trade‑off

Acknowledge impact → time‑boxed stabilization → single source of truth (war‑room doc). Hotfix (partition pruning), add expectations‑based data contracts, weekly KPI readouts. Confidence recovered in a week.

Discovery → roadmap
